On Reuse

Published on Fri 04 January 2013 under

I dream of feature re-use rather than code re-use. The "move line" feature in Eclipse is eminently practical and I miss it in all other text editors. I wish its algorithm was implemented only once on my machine and automatically applicable to any editable list of items (where a text is a list of lines). Jef Raskin was on to something.

Question: can a software environment with such level and granularity of re-use be built co-operatively by more than one person?
